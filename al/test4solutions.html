    1. \(f(e^4-3) = 2 + \ln (e^4-3+3) = 2 + 4 = 6\)
      \(g(6) = a(6^2) = 36a\)

    2. Let \(y = 2 + \ln(x+3)\)
      \(y - 2 = \ln(x+3)\)
      \(e^{y-2} = x+3\)
      \(x = e^{y-2}-3\)
      \(f^{-1}(x) = e^{x-2}-3\)
      The domain of \(f\) is \(x\geq 0\) so the range of \(f\) is \(f(x)\geq 2 + \ln 3\).
      The domain of \(f^{-1}\) is the range of \(f\) which is \(x \geq 2 + \ln 3\).

    Solve quadratic e.g. factorise \((\tan\theta 4)(3\tan\theta - 2\) or formula
    In the range of \(\pi/2 < \theta < \pi\), \(\tan\theta < 0\) so \(\tan\theta = -4\)
