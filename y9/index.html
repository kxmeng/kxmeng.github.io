Year 9 Maths Scheme of Work

Select a topic on the left

Resources

Ratio and Proportion (1.5 weeks)

  • Concept of a Ratio
  • Simplifying Ratios - including avoiding decimals and fractions or units in ratios
  • Sharing in a given ratio
  • If \(a:b\) and \(b:c\), find \(a:c\)
  • Direct & Inverse proportion - no constants of proportionality
  • Simple inverse problems that can be solved using rates and manhours e.g. workers painting walls, taps filling baths
  • Tree diagrams for “Half of books are about chess, two thirds of those are russian. What fraction of books are russian chess books?” etc.
  • Pink paint ratio demo
  • Ratio challenge questions
  • Mixed ratio proportion questions

Indices (1.5 weeks)

  • Basic Indices laws - multiplication, division, \((a^b)^c\)
  • Meaning of fractional and negative indices with justification
  • Evaluation of numbers in index form including fractions to negative indices
  • Writing numbers in index form
  • Solving index equations - including changing the base if time
  • Index laws review
  • Mixed questions
  • Indices questions
  • C1 Solomon indices

Equivalent Expressions (1.5 weeks)

  • Recap algebra basics - collecting like terms, multiplying one and two brackets
  • Identities vs. Equations
  • Factorisation into two (or more) brackets - include examples of the form \(pq + 2q + 4p + 8\) and difference of two squares, more able groups can think about non integer factorisations
  • Algebra review
  • Equating coefficients
  • Factorising single brackets
  • Harder linear factorisation
  • Factorising quadratics
  • Quadratic factorising - anagrams
