Year 11 Maths Scheme of Work

Select a topic on the left

Straight Lines

  • Review straight lines, including parallel perpendicular lines
  • Coordinate geometry (use A level questions)

Vectors (1.5 weeks)

  • Magnitude and direction
  • Notation, including representation as column and component form
  • Multiplication by scalar
  • Addition and subtraction, graphical interpretation, commutativity
  • Magnitude
  • Geometric proofs
  • 2 by 2 matrices: addition, multiplication, identity, inverse

Functions (2 weeks)

  • Mapping
  • Notation
  • Domain (and restrictions) and range
  • Composite and inverse
  • Inverse quadratic, including domain and choosing the appropriate square root
  • Sketching modulus functions
  • Solving modulus equations

HCF/LCM

  • Prime and algebraic factors

Differentiation (2 weeks)

  • Estimating gradient of curve by sketching tangent and finding gradient
  • Gradient of tangent as a rate of change
  • Graphical justification of differentiation (either by measuring gradients at multiple points or bounding the gradient of tangent)
  • Differentiating integer powers of x
  • Calculating the gradient of the tangent at a given point
  • Finding the equation of a tangent/normal at a given point
  • Finding points at which the tangent to the graph has a given gradient
  • Identifying co-ordinates of turning points; distinguishing between maxima and minima
  • Anti differentiation

Algebraic Fractions (1.5 weeks)

  • Factorising and simplifying
  • Multiplying and dividing
  • Adding and subtracting
  • Solving equations
  • Interesting denominators with LCM
  • C4 rational functions, basic partial fractions

Proportion (1.5 weeks)

  • Ratio and proportion with and without equations
  • Graphs

Quadratic Inequalities

  • Review quadratics
  • Inequalities by sketching

Units

  • Convert area and volume
  • Compound units e.g. density, pressure

Travel Graphs (1.5 weeks)

  • Travel graphs and interpretation
  • Calculus as rate of change, displacemet velocity acceleration
  • Displacement/distance, velocity/speed

Transformations (1.5 weeks)

  • Transformations of graphs, including trig and quadratics
  • Circle, reflecting graphs
  • Review transformations of shapes

Arithmetic Sequences (1.5 weeks)

  • Sequences and series, including proof
  • nth term expressions and equation
  • First term and common difference
  • Sum of first n terms

Geometry Review

  • Polygons, angles, circles, properties of shapes

Statistics Review

  • Histograms/cf diagrams, median, quartiles
  • Mean from grouped data

Constructions Review

  • Constructions and loci

Bounds Review

  • Upper and lower bounds
